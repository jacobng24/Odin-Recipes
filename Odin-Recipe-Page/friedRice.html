Jacob's Ramen Recipe

This is Jacob's fried rice recipe. zao shang hao zhong guo shen zai wo you bing chilling
Jacob eats fried rice, and it makes him very happy.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onions
  • A5 Wagyu
  • egg
Steps
  1. WASH THE RICE BEFORE YOU COOK IT PLEASE
  2. Cook the rice with the rice cooker
  3. Fry the rice and egg
  4. Add the other ingredients and make sure the meat is cooked
  5. Serve with bowl
