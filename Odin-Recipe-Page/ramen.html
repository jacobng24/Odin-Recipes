Jacob's Ramen Recipe

This is Jacob's Monkey Ramen Recipe. Jacob loves his ramen because he is cool.
He eats the ramen like a happy monkey

Ingredients

  • Maruchan Ramen Noodles
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onions
  • A5 Wagyu
  • A hint of banana
Steps
  1. Put the ramen in the pot with the boiling wata
  2. While the ramen cooks, take the A5 Wagyu and soy sauce and mix them together
  3. After the ramen cook stick da A5 Wagyu mix in the pot with the ramen
  4. Top with the ramen sauce and the green onions. Don't forget the bit of banana
  5. Eat like a happy monkey :)
